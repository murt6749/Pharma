Natural Products Quiz

1. Within natural product chemistry, which criterion primarily distinguishes a natural product from a synthetic compound?

a) Structural complexity
b) Pharmacological properties
c) Origin from a living source
d) Application in medicine

Detailed Explanation: Natural products are defined by their biological origin. Although many possess complex structures and useful pharmacological effects, the key distinction is that they are derived from living organisms.

2. A compound critical for a plant's basic metabolic functions is most accurately classified as:

a) A secondary metabolite

c) An alkaloid
d) A terpenoid

Detailed Explanation: Primary metabolites are essential for life, while secondary metabolites serve specialized functions such as defense or signaling.
